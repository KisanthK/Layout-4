Our Story

From the garden
to your skin

"I couldn't read the ingredients, and my skin was paying for it. So I made something I could actually trust."

Samisha started Blue Gardens after years of buying soap she couldn't understand, made with things that left her skin worse off. She went back to basics: Shea Butter, Olive Oil, Honey, Oats — ingredients you recognise, in bars slow-cured for six weeks. No fillers. No guesswork.

— Samisha, Owner
